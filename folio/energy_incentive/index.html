Energy Incentive Program Victoria – RDC

  • Energy Incentive Program Victoria is based on the Victorian Energy Efficiency Target scheme, a State Government initiative to reduce the greenhouse gas emissions and make energy efficient products more affordable for consumers

  • Our online claim form only takes 2 minutes to complete. You will also need to attach your proof of purchase and, if installation involved wiring, a certificate of electrical safety (COES)

  • Incentives are funded indirectly by large Victorian energy retailers via the Victorian Energy Efficiency Target scheme

  • This year your business can receive substantial financial incentives for purchasing High Energy Efficient RDCs. Incentives will vary by cabinet type, model and display area

  • By choosing high energy efficient RDCs your business will save hundreds of dollars off your energy bill every year! It won't be long before you will start realising the savings

  • Financial incentives are available on over 300 RDCs rated 'High Efficiency’ as per Australia Standard 1731

  • By switching to High Energy Efficient RDCs your business will contribute to the reduction of the greenhouse gas emissions in Australia
